We are family, friends, neighbors and co-workers, all with a shared interest in making Springfield the kindest place to call home. We believe in giving our time and resources to not just heal but to give back to a wonderful community that carried all of us through our time of need and sorrow.
LEARN MORE
Springfield Township is our home. Through the support of our community our focus is to help our very own.
Our kids are our priority when evaluating who to support. Small funds have been set up at various levels of our schools. The goal is provide kids who are at a financial disadvantage with the means that will allow them to participate in their school and extracurricular activities. Our hope is to assist our kids so their talents and their dreams are supported.
Springfield’s families are our family. We reach out to each other when things get hard. It could be as simple as providing a house cleaning, a cooked meal a lawn mowed. Sadly, much of our assistance comes when catastrophe strikes. It is our goal to take some of the financial strain off of our friends and neighbors.
Learn more about our community

Shane’s Kindness provides temporary support to help families and individuals. By providing families with the temporary means to participate in school, engage in their community and remain in their homes, we’re making sure every Springfield resident knows they are important and they matter.

Helping our students thrive by relieving some of the financial worry. Whether it’s money for supplies, transportation or team fees, we provide students with the opportunity to fulfill their potential. Money shouldn’t stand between our children and success.

We are committed to both the physical and the emotional needs of families. We will always try to be there quickly when life events prevent people from meeting monthly expenses. Counseling, tutoring, rent, mortgage and utility assistance. We are limited to how much we can provide, but it is our goal to provide breathing room in emergency situations.
